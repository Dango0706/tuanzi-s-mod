# Tuanzi's Mod 1.0.6 Changelog

## 26.2 Compatibility Upgrade & Bug Fixes
- Upgraded and adapted to Minecraft 26.2.
- Fixed compilation errors related to `EntityType` constant relocations to `EntityTypes`.
- Adapted and refactored stained glass panes and glazed terracotta references to the new `ColorCollection` API.
- Updated functional parameter interfaces for `PostSpawnProcessor` and `TeamColor`.
- Refactored client-side GUI hidden logic to use `hud.isHidden` and adapted to `Minecraft.setScreenAndShow`.
- Updated signature of the overridden `knockback` method in `LivingEntity`.
- Adjusted `OrderedSubmitNodeCollector.submitNameTag` parameter lists to remove the obsolete distance parameter.
- Successfully regenerated localizations, recipes, and tags using Fabric DataGen on version 26.2.

## New Weapons
- **Added New Weapon: Fractured Void Scar (裂虚之痕)**:
  - Fused by an End Knight from their shadow and shattered dragon breath fragments in the rift of the End.
  - Stats: Attack Damage 5.12, Attack Speed 2.0.
  - Passive "Void Cut": Deals 175% damage when the target has 0 armor. Otherwise, deals original damage.
  - Unique Wear Mechanic: Features 5 distinct appearance stages (Intangible, Ripple, Corrosion, Decaying, Echo) with smooth transition tints, particles, and custom sound pitch.
- **Added New Weapon: Steel Barrier (钢御壁垒)**:
  - The remnants of the last wall defender's sword. The smith forged the knight's soul and broken shield into the blade spine. When swung, a phantom steel shield wraps the wielder.
  - Stats: Attack Damage 7.7, Attack Speed 1.6.
  - Passive "Steel Shield": Every hit grants a layer of "Steel Shield" effect (max 5 layers). Each layer provides +2 armor (breaking vanilla limit). When duration (5s) expires, layers decay by 1 every 5s instead of disappearing instantly.
  - Unique Wear Mechanic: Features 5 distinct appearance stages (Pristine Forged, First Battle, Spreading Cracks, Mottled Rampart, Near Ruined Shield) with custom sounds depending on wear.

## New Enchantments
- **Added New Enchantment: Void Resonance (虚无共鸣)**:
  - Max level V, exclusive with other normalization enchantments (like Resonance Pulse, Abyssal Rhythm), and only applies to melee weapons.
  - Effect: When attacking a target with 0 armor, increases base damage by `(0.8 * level) * (attack speed / base weapon damage)`.
- **Added New Enchantment: Gift of the Shield (坚盾之赐 / Steel Shield Gift)**:
  - Max level IV, exclusive with other normalization enchantments, and applies to melee weapons and tools.
  - Effect: Grants `0.5 * level` armor when held in main hand. Additionally, when wielder's armor exceeds 20, each point of overflow armor increases attack damage by `0.0625 / 0.125 / 0.1875 / 0.25 * overflow_points`.

## New Items
- **Added New Item: Craftsman Charm (工匠护符)**:
  - A small metallic charm with crafting table wood grid textures and amethyst/obsidian trims. Emits a subtle purple foil glint when placed in inventories.
  - **Right-Click**: Opens a 3x3 portable crafting grid interface directly.
  - **Grid Caching**: Features 9 internal buffer slots. Retains all items inside on menu close instead of dropping them.
  - **JEI Transfer**: Fully supports JEI Recipe Transfer (one-click fill by clicking the "plus" button inside JEI recipe panel).
  - **Interaction Restriction & Safety**: The charm itself cannot be placed into its own buffer slots. Totally manual operation, no automation interaction. If the charm is moved out of the slot that opened the screen, the GUI closes automatically to prevent potential dupe exploits.
  - **Recipe**: 1 Crafting Table + 2 Obsidian + 1 Amethyst Shard + 1 Nether Quartz in shapeless crafting.

- **Added New Armor: Tidal Weave Boots (潮汐织靴)**:
  - A pair of lightweight boots woven from prismarine crystals and phantom membranes. The surface flows with a pulsing aura of the heart of the sea. Leaves wet footprints that quickly evaporate into tiny bubbles.
  - Stats: Identical to Diamond Boots (3 Armor, 2 Armor Toughness), 429 Durability. Can be repaired in an anvil using phantom membranes or prismarine crystals.
  - Core Passive "Water Walking": Walk, sprint, and jump freely on the surface of water as if it were a solid block when equipped in the feet slot.
  - Core Active "Diving Toggle": Press the sneak key (Shift) while walking on water to submerge into swimming. Release and swim back to the surface to automatically resume water walking.
  - Core Passive "Fall Mitigation & Light Bounce": Immune to all fall damage when falling onto the surface of water. Bounces lightly off the water surface upon landing from a height, generating splash and bubble particles.

- **Added New Item: World Sculptor's Pen (塑世之笔)**:
  - A luxury writing tool made of a netherite nib and an amethyst barrel, wrapped with glowing sculk-like fluorescent ink lines. While held, it leaves a transient light-blue trail in the air (using smaller glow particles, optimized with offset and reduced frequency to prevent blocking first-person view).
  - **Batch Fill**: Right-click a block to record the starting point. Then, right-click another block within a maximum straight distance of 64 blocks and a maximum volume of 2048 blocks to instantly fill the cuboid area with selected blocks.
  - **Block Selection**: Hold Shift + Right-click any block to set it as the active fill type. The selected block will be rendered as a micro-icon at the bottom-right of the pen's texture, allowing for quick preview.
  - **Smart Consumption**: Blocks are automatically deducted from the player's main inventory and shulker boxes inside. The operation is cancelled with a prompt if the block count or pen durability is insufficient.
  - **Undo Mechanic**: Within 60 seconds after a fill, sneak (Shift) + right-click (either in the air or on a block) to undo. Only blocks that remain unchanged are restored, returning the items to your inventory/shulker boxes and restoring the corresponding pen durability. Blocks with a hardness of -1 will be skipped during undo to ensure safety.
  - **Indestructible Blocks Limit**: Strictly forbids destroying, overriding, or placing blocks with a hardness of -1 (such as Bedrock, Barrier, etc.) in any fill mode (including AIR_ONLY mode). Blocks with a hardness less than 0 cannot be selected as fill types.
  - **Durability & Anvil Repair**: Features 8192 durability and cannot be enchanted. Can be repaired in an anvil using a single Void Ink Ingot for a fixed 1000 durability points.
  - **Acquisition**: Temporarily unavailable in survival mode.

- **Added New Item: Void Ink Ingot (虚空墨锭)**:
  - A deep purple ink ingot pressed from condensed dragon's breath and echo shards, with eddying patterns like the End void flowing on its surface.
  - Used as the unique repair material for the World Sculptor's Pen in an anvil (restores 1000 durability per ingot).
  - **Anvil Incompatibility Red Warning**: Placing other tools or armors with Void Ink Ingot in an anvil displays "Ink Incompatible" (using redstone dust as indicator) in the output slot. Experience cost is reset to 0, and players cannot pick it up, achieving native-style repair blocking.
  - **Acquisition**: Temporarily unavailable in survival mode, but added to the mod creative tab for testing.

## Mechanism Optimization
- **Optimized Soul Merchant Station Restock Timing**:
  - Changed the restock detection cycle from relying on game time (originally 24000 ticks) to being **driven by the BlockEntity's own internal Tick counter**. The timer increments when the block entity is loaded and contains a villager, triggering a restock and resetting once it reaches 8000 ticks (equivalent to 8 hours of game time).
  - This completely resolves issues where player sleeping or using the `/time set` command skipped or warped game time, causing trading restocks to skip or trigger incorrectly.
  - Added NBT serialization for `RestockTimer` to ensure progress is properly retained when chunks unload or the server restarts.
  - Added logic-based debug logging to output restock triggers in the console.

## New Feature: Blueprint Construction System
- **Added Block: Blueprint Cannon (蓝图大炮)**:
  - A precision machine block for automated construction in survival mode. Builds a structure step-by-step according to a recorded Structure Blueprint.
  - **Efficiency**: Automatically places blocks every 5 ticks.
  - **Material Scanning**: Automatically scans chests, barrels, and shulkers within a 5-block radius. Supports recursive scanning up to 3 levels deep (e.g. shulker boxes inside chests). Auto-pauses and emits warning sound if blocks are missing.
  - **Fuel Source**: Consumes 1 durability of Compressed Build Slurry per block placed/broken in survival mode. Free in creative mode.
  - **Multi-mode support**: GUI allows start, pause, terminate actions, block mismatch destruction (drop vs. void modes), and exact NBT matching toggle.
- **Added Block: Blueprint Table (蓝图桌)**:
  - Tech table for importing and exporting blueprints.
  - **Export**: Place a recorded Structure Blueprint in the table to export it as a standard `.litematic` file inside `blueprints/exports/`.
  - **Import**: Automatically scans `blueprints/imports/` for `.litematic` or Sponge `.schem` files. Converts a Blank Blueprint in the slot into a Structure Blueprint, and renders a miniature rotating 3D ghost preview.
- **Added Item: Compressed Build Slurry (压缩建造浆料)**:
  - Fuel source for the cannon. Shapeless recipe: 1 Blaze Powder + 4 Redstone + 4 Gravel. Each slurry provides 64 durability.
- **Added Items: Blank & Structure Blueprint (空白/结构蓝图)**:
  - **Selection Recording**: Right-click two corners of a structure using a Blank Blueprint, then click the [Confirm Construction] link in chat to consume one Blank Blueprint and record the block state, palette, and NBT layout (supports volumes up to 262,144 blocks).
  - **Ghost Control**: Right-click a Structure Blueprint to project a 0.4 alpha transparent BlockDisplay ghost preview. Shortcuts: R to rotate, F to mirror, Ctrl + Arrow Keys/PageUp/PageDown to adjust offset/height. Press V to hide/show, G to open the floating menu.
  - **Material List Refactoring**: The material list is now printed into a book. Place a "Book and Quill" (writable book) in the third slot of the Blueprint Cannon and click the "List" button; the cannon will scan inventories within 5 blocks and output a written book containing the materials list.
  - **Formatted Materials List**: The book lists item demands clearly categorized, with format `✗ Item Name: Available/Total` (Red for missing) and `✔ Item Name: Available/Total` (Green for sufficient).
  - **Robust Localization & Deprecated Screen**: Rewrote the book text generator engine to use rich-text Component rather than calling raw getString() on server. This ensures the item names are translated on client side in their active local language instead of raw translation keys. The old overlapping Material List screen has been safely renamed to `.bak` and archived.

## Blueprint Construction System Deep Optimization
- **Unified Import & Export Directory**: Merged the previous import (`blueprints/imports`) and export (`blueprints/exports`) directories into a single unified `blueprints` folder for more centralized file management.
- **Fuel Mechanism Refactoring**: The build fuel count is now stored within the Blueprint Cannon's block entity state rather than relying on item durability values. Compressed Build Slurry items no longer have durability properties and can now stack up to 64.
- **Cannon GUI Alignments & Symbol Buttons**: Corrected structural overlapping bugs in the Blueprint Cannon Screen. Replaced wordy action buttons with tiny graphical symbol buttons (▶, ‖, ■) for a cleaner layout. Added a long progress bar spanning the GUI width, and implemented vivid red error indicators for block/fuel shortages.
- **Blueprint Table GUI & Sidebar 3D Isometric Preview**: Fixed asymmetry in the Blueprint Table Screen. Side lists are now rendered inside an attached scrolling sidebar. When "Preview" is selected, the sidebar seamlessly transitions into a high-performance pseudo-3D isometric renderer displaying a rotating miniature voxel structure of the target blueprint.
- **High-Fidelity 3D Block Rendering in Preview**: Refactored the pseudo-3D preview renderer to display actual correct blocks (their 3D models and textures) instead of plain single-colored cubes. Fluid blocks (water/lava) and other non-item technical blocks have realistic visual fallback mappings. Implemented a smart performance fallback: when scaled down extremely small (scale < 1.5f) or displaying giant structures, the renderer automatically falls back to super-fast flat pixel rendering, paired with optimized block thinning to ensure 100% smooth frame rates.
- **Blockbench 3D Voxel Models**: Fully rebuilt the block and item model shapes of the Blueprint Cannon and Blueprint Table in Blockbench using vanilla-aligned low-poly cubes to match Minecraft's original voxel art style.
- **Fixed Blueprint Table Isometric Preview Depth Sorting & 3D Perspective Upgrade**: Corrected the sign error in the Y-axis (height) depth sorting formula. Furthermore, upgraded the rendering projection model by introducing focal distance and implementing a true 3D perspective projection (near-large, far-small scale with depth parallax), significantly enhancing the spatial 3D fidelity and height rendering precision during preview rotation.
- **Blueprint Cannon Fluid Coverage & Barrier Exclusion Optimizations**: Improved the Blueprint Cannon's behavior when encountering fluids; it now bypasses fluid destruction and directly places blocks to cover them. Additionally, barrier blocks within the blueprint's outline/boundaries are now completely ignored and treated as air (they will not be queried as materials, placed in survival, or rendered in 3D previews/world ghost outlines).
- **Fixed Blueprint Cannon Failing to Deduct Items from Shulker Boxes in Chests**: Fixed a bug where the Blueprint Cannon could correctly identify items stored inside shulker boxes within chests, but failed to deduct them. The root cause was that `ContainerSource.markChanged()`, when the item was located inside a shulker box's virtual container, only called `setChanged()` on the temporary virtual container (which has no persistence effect). The fix serializes the updated virtual container contents back into the parent shulker box's `DataComponents.CONTAINER` NBT component and propagates the change up to the real chest block entity, ensuring item deductions are fully persisted.
- **Fixed Shulker Box Contents Being Replaced by Chest Contents**: Fixed a new bug introduced by the previous shulker fix: after serializing the virtual container's contents to the parent shulker box's NBT, `markChanged()` incorrectly called the parent's `markChanged()`, which internally read from `parent.container` (the real chest container rather than the virtual container), causing the entire chest's contents to overwrite the shulker box's NBT. Fixed by introducing a new `propagateChangedToRoot()` method that correctly walks up the parent chain — serializing each layer's virtualContainer to the layer above's shulkerStack — until reaching the root chest node where it calls `setChanged()`.
- **Fixed Completing Blueprint Then Immediately Finishing After Switching**: Fixed a bug where after the Blueprint Cannon finishes building, switching to a different blueprint and pressing Start would immediately report completion. The root cause was that `handleAction("START")` did not reset `currentIndex` after task completion, leaving it at the end of the old blueprint. On the first tick with the new blueprint, `currentIndex >= blocks.length` was immediately satisfied, triggering a false completion. Fixed by resetting `currentIndex=0` and `placedBlocks=0` when starting from IDLE state (buildState==0, including after completion); resuming from PAUSE does not reset them, preserving the previous progress.
- **Fixed Blueprint Cannon Progress Bar Incorrectly Displayed**: Refactored the progress calculation approach and fixed multiple related issues. The old implementation calculated progress as `placedBlocks (actual blocks placed) / Width×Height×Length (total grid cells including air)` — since the denominators differed, progress could never reach 100%. Replaced with a unified `currentIndex / totalBlocks` formula where `totalBlocks` is the actual length of the blocks array, ensuring `currentIndex == totalBlocks` produces exactly 100% on completion. Additional improvements: when the blueprint is removed from the slot, `totalBlocks` is immediately synced to 0 so the progress bar clears and displays "No Blueprint"; progress bar color now dynamically changes — green while running, orange while paused, gray while idle. The deprecated `placedBlocks` field was removed entirely; progress data is now synced via ContainerData slot 1 (currentIndex) and slot 2 (totalBlocks), eliminating client-side blueprint NBT parsing.
- **Fixed Blueprint Cannon Placing Signs with Missing Text**: Fixed a bug where signs placed by the Blueprint Cannon did not show text. The root cause was that after loading block entity NBT data via `newBe.loadWithComponents` on the server, `level.sendBlockUpdated` was not called to sync the block entity state to nearby clients. Added the sync invocation after NBT loading to ensure block entities (like signs, decorated pots, etc.) update dynamically.
