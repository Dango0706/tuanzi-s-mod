# Tuanzi's Mod 1.0.3 Changelog

## New Features
- **New Material: Warden Heart**:
  - **Rare Drop**: Defeating a Warden has a **25%** chance to drop a Warden Heart (affected by Looting, +10% probability per level).
  - **Core Ingredient**: An essential material for forging the ultimate defensive weapon — **Echo Breaker**.
- **New Weapon/Shield: Echo Breaker**:
  - **Crafting Recipe**: Forged using 1 Warden Heart, 3 Echo Shards, and 1 Stick at a Crafting Table.
  - **Passive Property (Sonic Absorption)**: Effective only when held in the main hand. Automatically **reduces Sonic Boom damage by 30%** when attacked by a Warden.
  - **Active Skill (Perfect Block)**: Right-click to block for **0.5 seconds**, and can only be used when the **offhand is empty**. Grants **100% immunity** to Sonic Boom damage during the blocking window.
  - **Perfect Deflection (Sonic Reflection)**: Deflecting a Sonic Boom perfectly in the blocking window deflects the sound wave back to its source, dealing **15 true damage** and applying strong knockback to the Warden.
  - **Block Cost**: Each successful block consumes an extra **3 durability points** (total **4** points).
  - **Block Cooldown**: Triggers a **5.0-second** cooldown on **failure** (no damage deflected); triggers an **8.0-second** cooldown on **success** (perfectly blocked & deflected).
  - **Equipment Repair**: Can be repaired in an anvil using a **Warden Heart**.
- **New Enchantment: Smelting**:
  - **Core Function**: Skip the hassle of returning home to use a furnace. When mining blocks that can be smelted, it directly drops the smelted results.
  - **Full Compatibility**: Perfectly compatible with **Fortune**, allowing for increased smelted drops.
  - **Penalty**: The high temperature generated by the enchantment accelerates tool wear. Each smeltable block mined consumes **1 extra** durability (total **2** points per block).
  - **Applicable To**: Pickaxe, Axe, Shovel.
  - **Conflict**: Mutually exclusive with **Silk Touch**; they cannot coexist on the same tool.
  - **Acquisition**: A "Treasure Enchantment" that cannot be obtained from the enchantment table.
    - **Exploration**: May be found in chests within **Nether Fortresses** or **Desert Temples**.
    - **Trading**: Rarely offered by **Master-level Librarians** (costs 40-64 emeralds).
- **New Enchantment: Chain Mining**:
  - **Core Function**: Hold the activation key (default `~` / Grave Accent key) while mining to instantly mine surrounding connected blocks of the same type.
  - **Mining Limits**: Each level mines up to 16 blocks (max Level IV mines up to 64 blocks).
  - **Durability Cost**: Each chained block consumes tool durability normally. The chain stops safely if the tool breaks.
  - **Hunger Cost**: Each extra block mined adds **0.25** exhaustion. The exhaustion is accumulated and applied instantly to deduct your saturation and food level (capped at 40F exhaustion per tick, i.e., maximum 10 saturation/food points deduction per action), eliminating tick-deduction delay.
  - **Active HUD Overlay**: When Chain Mining is active (holding the keybind while wielding a tool with Chain Mining), a F3-style transparent gray overlay showing "Chain Mining Active" is rendered in the top-left corner, with "Active" highlighted in light green (§a).
  - **Acquisition**: Creative mode only; currently unavailable in survival mode.
- **New Item: Scarecrow**:
  - **Shapeless Crafting**: Crafted using 1 Hay Block, 1 Stick, and 1 Carved Pumpkin.
  - **Base Ingredient**: An essential defensive and decorative prop, also acting as the indispensable core ingredient to craft the advanced Decoy Totem.
  - **Major Visual Overhaul**:
    - **Visual Framework Perfection (No Floaty Parts, Oak Fence Pillar)**: 
      - **Oak Fence Center Post**: Completely hid the modern and out-of-place default dual wooden stand legs and stone baseplate of the ArmorStand. Replacing it with a high-fidelity, beautifully textured **Oak Fence** block rendered underneath at height `[-0.35, 0.65]` (deeply dug into the ground and meeting the hay block seamlessly), forming a classic single-post rustic wooden framework.
      - **Perfect Tight Fit**: Shifted the 3D Hay Block's rendering Y-level offset from `0.45F` to `0.65F` (height span `[0.65, 1.65]`). This allows the top of the hay block to fit perfectly inside and cover the bottom of the pumpkin head (~1.60F), sealing any floaty neck/head gaps!
      - **Natural Arm Extension**: The sideways horizontally-extended arms (at height 1.375F) now emerge naturally and elegantly from the upper-middle section of the Hay Block's side panels, eliminating any zombie-like look or disconnecting issues under the neck.
    - **Premium Boat-like Durability & Wiggling Physical Feedback**: Completely abolished the fragile one-hit instant-break mechanism, refactoring it to behave like a **Boat** with accumulated damage durability and smooth cooldown decay.
      - **Durable and Satisfying**: In survival mode, players need to hit the scarecrow 10 times with bare hands (or a few times with weapons) to break and retrieve it. Hit damage points smoothly decay at 0.1F/tick when not attacked.
      - **Wood Impact Sounds**: Every single strike triggers satisfying wood impact sound events (`WOOD_HIT`).
      - **Physical Wiggling**: Striking the scarecrow triggers a dynamic event (`byte 32`) that causes the entire entity (including the pumpkin head, 3D hay block, and extended arms) to sway and wiggle left and right smoothly, offering superb physical strike feedback!
      - **Creative Mode Convenience**: Hitting the scarecrow in creative mode still immediately breaks/discards it for instant clearing.
    - **Diurnal Jack-o'-Lantern Transition & Night Light Emission**: The scarecrow's head automatically transforms into a glowing **Jack-o'-Lantern** at night (when the sky darkens), and places an invisible **Light Block** with brightness level **7** at the head position, genuinely illuminating the surrounding area! It automatically transitions back to a normal **Carved Pumpkin** when day arrives, removing the light source. The light block is also automatically cleaned up when the scarecrow is broken or retrieved, leaving no residual data. A safety recovery drop filter prevents duplicate Jack-o'-lantern drops during night disassembly.
      - **Pumpkin Head Precision Alignment**: The pumpkin head model position has been shifted slightly upward so it sits perfectly flush with the top of the hay block, eliminating any gap.
  - **Completely Stationary**: The entity is fully stationary, meaning it cannot be pushed by other entities, nor will it be affected by gravity or fluid currents, providing rock-solid placement.
  - **Easy Recovery**: Players can retrieve the scarecrow by sneaking (Secondary Use) and right-clicking the entity, or by simply hitting it to break it. Recovery will safely drop the Scarecrow item along with any extra armor or items equipped on it by the player without loss.
- **New Consumable: Decoy Totem**:
  - **Shapeless Crafting**: Crafted using 1 Scarecrow and 1 Glowstone Dust.
  - **Active Skill (Summon Phantom)**: Right-click to spawn a player phantom in front of the player lasting **15 seconds**, triggering a 15-second item cooldown.
  - **Perfect Disguise**: The phantom looks exactly like the player at the moment of spawn (including skin, mainhand/offhand items, and armor set).
  - **Data Inheritance**: The phantom's health and armor values are perfectly copied from the player at the moment of spawn.
  - **Passive Agro Taunt**: Every 5 ticks, the phantom automatically draws agro from all hostiles (Mobs) within a **10-block** radius.
  - **Lifelike Player Behavior**: The phantom randomly performs idle, walk, and run movements. **The head no longer remains stiffly locked to the player's look direction at the moment of spawn (e.g., staring straight down if spawned while looking down). Instead, the phantom possesses an active, independent head-look animation, allowing it to smoothly look around or nod/tilt its head (larger yaw/pitch freedom during idle/walk, focused forward look with subtle head tilting during runs), making it look incredibly alive.** Random jumping for no reason has been completely removed. The phantom will only jump to navigate obstacles (horizontal collision) or execute bunny-hops with a 5% chance while in a sprint (Run) state. Sprinting while in the air grants a **20% speed boost**, providing highly natural parkour and tactical escape visuals.
  - **Fleeing Mechanism**: When hurt by any damage source, the phantom immediately panics and flees away from the damage source, executing a large escape jump forward!
  - **Shatter & Smoke Revenge**: Upon expiring after 15 seconds or health dropping to zero, the phantom vanishes instantly to avoid any red damage tinting or fall-over death animation. It shatters with a clay pot sound, producing a large burst of beautiful **white smoke and Poof particles**, and enrages hostiles within a **10-block** radius, granting them **Speed II** for **10 seconds**.
- **New Feature: Stair Seating**:
  - **Core Function**: Players can right-click any normally placed stairs (`half=bottom`) to sit on them, enjoying a seamless furniture interaction experience.
  - **Automatic Dismount Cleanup**: Custom-designed an extremely lightweight, invisible seat entity (`SeatEntity`). The server automatically detects if there are no passengers, and once the player dismounts (via pressing `Shift`, teleportation, disconnection, or death), the entity is immediately `discard()`ed. This ensures absolutely no ghost entities are left behind in the world.
  - **Suffocation and Stacking Prevention**: Intelligently checks if the block directly above the stairs has a solid collision shape to prevent players from suffocating when sitting. It also includes an overlapping check that prevents multiple players/entities from sitting on the exact same stair block at the same time.
  - **Seamless Compatibility with Normal Play**: Players will not trigger sitting when sneaking (Shift + right-click), right-clicking with offhand items, or when the stairs are upside down (`half=top`), allowing adjacent blocks to be placed normally. If the stair block is broken while sat upon, the player is safely dismounted and the entity is automatically destroyed.
- **New Enchantment: Blood Rage**:
  - **Furious Harvest**: Killing any mob while holding a weapon enchanted with Blood Rage (Swords & Axes only) grants the player the **Blood Rage** status effect for **7 seconds** with a custom dark-red aura icon texture. Consecutive kills will refresh the duration but will not stack the amplifier level.
  - **Combat Surge**: During Blood Rage, all outgoing attack damage dealt by the player is increased by **40%** (multiplicative).
  - **Defense Collapse**: As a cost, all incoming damage received by the player is also increased by **30%** (multiplicative).
  - **Fateful Backlash**: If the Blood Rage duration expires naturally without any new kills to refresh the timer, the player will immediately suffer **2 points (1 heart)** of feedback damage. This feedback damage has absolute true properties: it bypasses all armor, is not reduced by Protection enchantments, and does not trigger the 30% Blood Rage incoming damage vulnerability. Dying to this backlash displays a unique death message: "`xxxx died of Blood Rage backlash`".
  - **Applicability & Conflicts**: Applies to Swords and Axes, with a maximum level of I. It is mutually exclusive with **Fire Aspect** but perfectly compatible with **Sharpness**, **Smite**, and **Bane of Arthropods**.
  - **Acquisition**: Can be obtained directly from the Enchanting Table, salvaged from random dungeon chest loot, or traded with Librarian Villagers.
- **New Enchantment: Berserker**:
  - **Core Function**: When wearing the enchanted chestplate, melee attack damage is boosted based on the proportion of lost health. For every **10%** max health lost, melee damage increases by **5% (Level I)** or **8% (Level II)**. The boost reaches its cap when health falls below **5%** (i.e., 95% max health lost).
  - **Side Effect**: As a penalty, the chestplate's base armor value is reduced by **35% (Level I)** or **30% (Level II)** (meaning the chestplate still retains 65% or 70% of its base armor value. For example, a Diamond Chestplate with 8 base armor will only lose 2 points under Level II, still providing 6 armor points, instead of being reduced to only 30% armor).
  - **Scope**: Effective only on melee attacks (Swords, Axes, Trident melee attacks, Mace smash, etc.). The damage boost decreases in real-time if health is restored by healing.
  - **Exclusion**: Mutually exclusive with all protection enchantments including **Protection**, **Fire Protection**, **Blast Protection**, **Projectile Protection**, and **Feather Falling**.
  - **Removal & Dismount**: The armor penalty and damage boost are **immediately and automatically restored** if the chestplate is broken, unequipped, or when the enchantment is removed in a Grindstone.
  - **Visual Effect**: When wearing a Berserker chestplate and health falls below **30%**, a smooth dark-red vignette blood vessels effect fades in around the screen edges, intensifying as health drops closer to 5%, warning the player of their critical state.
  - **Acquisition**: Max level II. Obtainable via Enchanting Table (rare weight 2), random loot chests, or Librarian Villager trades.
- **New Utility Entity/Item: Trial Dummy**:
  - **Crafting Recipe**: Crafted using 5 Hay Blocks, 2 Sticks, 1 Scarecrow, and 1 Target block at a Crafting Table (the Scarecrow is placed in the center, other ingredients arranged symmetrically; see JEI for details).
  - **Placement**: Right-click on top of any solid block. To preserve server performance, a player can have at most 1 Trial Dummy within a 10-block radius. Placing a second one instantly and safely retrieves the first one without loss!
  - **Safe Recovery**: Sneak and right-click the dummy with an empty hand to recover it as an item. Recovery consumes 1 durability point (the item has 32 durability in total). If durability reaches 0, the item breaks and drops basic materials (0-2 sticks and 1 hay block). Upon recovery, all equipped armors and hand-held items drop safely without loss!
  - **Invulnerability & Defense Inheritance**: The dummy entity is completely invulnerable to environmental damage (except Void and creative-mode instant destruction). Players can right-click to dress or swap any armor/handheld items on it. The dummy inherits all armor points, toughness, protection enchantments, and active effects from its equipped gear, providing a 100% realistic entity defense calculation for incoming attacks!
  - **Weapon & Ammo Protection**: Wielding any weapon/tool to strike the dummy does not consume any tool durability! Shooting it with a bow/crossbow automatically recovers the arrow at the impact location, resulting in zero arrow consumption!
  - **Floating Damage Indicators**: Striking the dummy triggers 3D billboard-rendered orange floating damage digits. The text drifts down like a feather, bounces slightly on the ground, and fades out within 1.5 seconds. Consecutive hits smoothly offset the text to avoid overlapping.
  - **Real-Time DPS Stats & Query**:
    - **HUD Status**: Attacking the dummy starts a DPS session, rendering "⚔ Total XX.X | DPS: XX.X" on the player's Action Bar every second. If no attack occurs for 4 seconds, the test stops, archives, and prompts a reset message.
    - **Detailed Summary**: Right-click the dummy with an empty hand (without sneaking) to print a comprehensive summary in the chat, detailing total damage, max single strike, hit count, average DPS, and session duration!
  - **JEI Description**: Includes a fully detailed JEI guide explaining placement rules, armor logic, and stats.
  - **Chest Loot**: Spawns in Pillager Outposts and Village Weaponsmith chests with a high probability of 20%.

## Creative Mode & JEI Support

- **JEI Description Integration**: Added custom JEI info description pages for "Scarecrow" and "Decoy Totem", allowing players to easily look up recipes, mechanics, and usage details in JEI.

## Adjustments & Optimizations
- **Fix Chain Mining Hunger Synced Issue**:
  - Resolved the issue where Chain Mining hunger exhaustion was not correctly synced and updated on the client. Now it immediately sends a status sync packet (`ClientboundSetHealthPacket`) to the client, guaranteeing that the HUD food/saturation bars are updated instantly.
- **Fix Smelting and Fortune Conflict**:
  - Resolved the issue where the Smelting enchantment incorrectly conflicted with Fortune. Now they are fully compatible, can coexist on a single tool, and will properly drop multiple smelted outputs when under the influence of Fortune.
- **Warden Combat Runtime Crash Fix**:
  - Refactored the underlying Mixin entry point design. Migrated the original `@Redirect` damage redirection logic to a highly secure head-injection intercept in `LivingEntity.hurtServer`.
  - Completely resolved the game runtime crash caused by Lambda synthetic method matching failures during Warden combat or summoning (`Mixin transformation of net.minecraft.world.entity.ai.behavior.warden.SonicBoom failed`), greatly improving mod stability and compatibility in complex combat scenarios.
- **Damage Calculation Refactoring**:
  - Introduced the centralized `DamageCalculator` utility class to manage and calculate all mod-related damage modifications.
  - Refactored "Blood Rage" damage boosts and vulnerability calculations to leverage this utility.
  - Supports an **additive (Additive)** damage calculation model for multiple concurrent modifiers (e.g., if a player has Blood Rage (+40%) and a future +20% damage buff, they will accumulate to a +60% total damage increase instead of being applied multiplicatively).
- **Decoy Totem Recipe Refactored**:
  - The Decoy Totem recipe has been refactored from a simple shapeless crafting (Scarecrow + Glowstone Dust) to a highly tactical, shaped recipe: Top row [Empty, Sculk Vein, Empty], Middle row [Phantom Membrane, Scarecrow, Phantom Membrane], Bottom row [Glowstone, Redstone, Glowstone] at a Crafting Table. JEI descriptions have been fully updated.
- **Trial Dummy Armor & Enchantment Inheritance Fixed**:
  - Completely resolved the critical bug where armors and enchantments equipped on the Trial Dummy failed to calculate and reduce incoming damage. The dummy now correctly behaves as a standard `LivingEntity`, correctly reducing damage according to armor values, toughness, potions, and active enchantments to provide a 100% accurate DPS testing environment!
- **Undying Potion Restrictions**:
  - The "Undying" status effect now only applies to **Players** and **Tamed Mobs** (e.g., tamed wolves, cats).
  - It no longer works on wild or hostile mobs, preventing accidental protection of enemies.
- **Expanded JEI Support**:
  - Registered detailed JEI encyclopedic description guides for the new **Smelting** enchantment, **Warden Heart**, and **Echo Breaker** in both English and Chinese, making it convenient for players to query drop rates, crafting methods, and defensive/reflective attribute values.
