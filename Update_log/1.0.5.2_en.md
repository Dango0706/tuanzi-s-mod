# Tuanzi's Mod 1.0.5.2 Changelog

## Fixes

- **Fixed Scully Shard and Tide Cleaver Not Being Recognized as Swords in Enchanting Tables and Anvils**:
  - **Issue Details**: The newly added weapons "Scully Shard" and "Tide Cleaver" were missing from the vanilla swords item tag (`minecraft:swords`). This caused enchanting tables to offer no sword-specific enchantments for them, and prevented anvils from applying enchanted books or repairing them with their respective repair materials.
  - **Fix**: Added `SCULLY_SHARD` and `TIDE_CLEAVER` to the vanilla `ItemTags.SWORDS` tag in the DataGen item tag provider, allowing the enchanting table and anvil mechanics to correctly recognize them as swords, enabling all standard sword enchantments (such as Sharpness, Knockback, Looting) and repair functions.
